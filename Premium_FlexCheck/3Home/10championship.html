🏁 How to Win (End of Month)

🗳️ One winner per tier is selected each week by vote.
Those weekly winners enter head-to-head judged battles.
🏆 The final showdown crowns Three Monthly Champions.

  • Each week, one winner per tier (Top, Mid, Bot) is chosen by community vote and advances to that tier’s monthly bracket.
  • The bracket follows a single-elimination format:
    • Week 1 Winner vs Week 2 Winner → Semifinal 1
    • Week 3 Winner vs Week 4 Winner → Semifinal 2
    • Winners of each semifinal face off in the Championship Final
  • Semifinals and Championship are LIVE judged battles (7pm CT) during FlexCheck Friday livestreams.
    • Judged directly by me — no polls, no audience vote
    • Winners are chosen based on physique, effort, presentation, and improvement
  • The Championship Final takes place on the last Friday of the month and crowns one winner per tier.
  • 3 Total Monthly Champions are selected — one from Top, Mid, and Bot Tier.
  • Monthly Champions earn:
    • A FREE Priority Physique Check
    • A permanent spot in the FlexCheck Hall of Fame
